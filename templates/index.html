AED Finder

An automated external defibrillator (AED) is a portable electronic device that automatically diagnoses the life-threatening disease of heart in emergency situations.

There are around 1400 AED located in various parts of Hong Kong. It is important to be able to find an AED quickly in an emergency.

Intelligent Q&A Medical System

QA system is used for users who encounter some medical-related questions. users stay at home to learn something about how to prevent and treat diseases.

Users can describe the symptoms, ask some questions about diseases and healthcare. Then the system can Automatic classify and identify the problems to give some advises from medical knowledge graph

  • Symptoms
  • Treatments
  • Treatment cycle
  • Healing rate
  • Check items
  • Department
  • Possible illnesses for the symptoms
